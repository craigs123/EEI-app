About the Ecosystem Integrity Index (EEI)

The EEI is a composite metric measuring ecosystem health on a scale of 0 (degraded) to 1 (pristine). It combines three key components:

  • Functional Integrity - Net Primary Productivity compared to natural baseline
  • Structural Integrity - Landscape connectivity and human modification
  • Compositional Integrity - Biodiversity Intactness Index (BII)
